# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard developed using Microsoft Power BI. It analyzes employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary distribution, job roles, education background, and years at the company to help HR teams identify attrition patterns and support data-driven workforce decisions.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Microsoft Excel

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- Total Employees KPI
- Total Attrition KPI
- Attrition Rate
- Average Employee Age
- Average Salary
- Average Years at Company
- Attrition by Education
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Salary Slab
- Attrition by Years at Company
- Attrition by Job Role
- Attrition by Gender
- Department Filter (HR, R&D, Sales)

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- The organization has *1,470 employees*.
- Total employee attrition is *237, resulting in an **attrition rate of 16%*.
- The average employee age is *37 years*.
- The average monthly salary is *6.5K*.
- Employees have an average tenure of *7 years*.
- The *Life Sciences* education background has the highest attrition.
- Employees aged *26–35 years* show the highest attrition.
- The *Up to 5K* salary slab has the highest employee attrition.
- *Laboratory Technicians* experience the highest attrition among job roles.
- Male employees have higher attrition than female employees.
  
---

## 🎯 Business Recommendations
- Improve employee retention for the *26–35 age group* through career development programs.
- Review compensation for employees in the *Up to 5K salary slab*.
- Focus retention initiatives on *Laboratory Technicians* and other high-attrition job roles.
- Analyze the reasons behind higher attrition among employees with *Life Sciences* education backgrounds.
- Conduct regular employee engagement and satisfaction surveys to reduce attrition.
